People often ask me: "Joseph, you're a CISO. Why are you spending your time writing a DOCX stamper in Java?"

### The Invisible Infrastructure
Document generation is one of those "invisible" pieces of infrastructure that everyone relies on but nobody wants to fix. In high-stakes environments, documents are the legal and operational "source of truth." If they are insecure or unreliable, the whole system collapses.

### My Mission
My mission with `office-stamper` isn't just to make a tool; it's to prove that even the most "boring" parts of enterprise software deserve **excellence**. Whether it's the security of a SpEL expression or the crispness of an SVG logo, it all contributes to a culture of quality.
