With the stability of the v3.3.0 release, it's time to start talking about **v4**. 

### The Vision
The v3 series was about modularization, SpEL safety, and API consolidation. For v4, I want to focus on:
- **Streaming Document Generation**: Moving beyond the DOM-based model to handle massive documents with minimal memory overhead.
- **Enhanced AI Integration**: Built-in hooks for common LLM providers to automate the "JSON to Template" pipeline.
- **Native AOT Compilation**: Better support for GraalVM to make the CLI tool start instantly.

### We Need Your Voice
`office-stamper` has always been a project driven by real-world needs. Whether you're in a highly regulated industry or just need to generate simple invoices, your feedback is what shapes our roadmap.

**Questions for the next round:**
- Joseph, is 'Streaming' the right technical priority, or is 'Cloud-Native/Serverless' a better hook for the v4 narrative?
- Should we announce a formal 'Contributor Program' or keep the current sporadic model?
