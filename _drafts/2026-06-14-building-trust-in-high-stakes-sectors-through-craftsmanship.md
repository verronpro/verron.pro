In the nuclear energy sector, trust isn't built on promises; it's built on verifiable evidence. The same should be true for software. 

### Verifiable Quality
When I talk about "Craftsmanship," I'm referring to the technical choices that make a system resilient. For `office-stamper`, this means:
- **Mutation Testing (PIT)**: We don't just aim for 100% line coverage; we use mutation testing to ensure our tests are actually *effective* at catching bugs.
- **Strict APIs**: By consolidating our API in v3.1 and v3.2, we've reduced the surface area for misuse.
- **Continuous Quality**: Integrated Qodana analysis ensures every commit meets our "Caring Coder" standard.

### The Real-World Impact
When an automated system generates a compliance report for a reactor's structural integrity, there is zero room for error. Craftsmanship is what ensures that the document produced is exactly what the data dictated—no more, no less.
